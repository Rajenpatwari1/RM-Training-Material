Employee Training Guide
Client Script & Compliance

Essential communication, service, and regulatory guidelines.

What to Say (The Script)

Introductory Calls

  1. I am calling from OnePaper Research Analysts Private Limited.
  2. We are a SEBI and B S E Registered Research Analyst Company and provide research services.
  3. May I know how's your trading going on?

Probing Questions

  1. In which segment you are doing trading and investment?
  2. How much experience you have in share market?
  3. You are self-trader or taking recommendations from someone?
  4. How much capital you are maintaining and how's your trading going on like you're in profit or loss?

Sales Pitch

  1. Greetings, I am from OnePaper Research Analysts, we had a chat a short while ago. I am calling up to follow up on that.
  2. Did you go through our website? In either case, I would just like to take some time to tell you about the products that we are offering for you to make a better and more informed decision regarding the services that you may wish to avail from us.
  3. If you have any questions regarding the services provided by us or any other aspect then please feel free to ask.
  4. I am also sharing the bank account details with you regarding the payment to be made.
  5. We would be very happy to have you as a customer. Please feel to reach out in case of any further queries or assistance required.

What to Offer (Our Services)

Standard Services

Stock Cash

GUIDELINES FOR RESTRICTIONS FOR CLIENT COMMUNICATION ON CALLS (Strict Compliance Rules)

1. No Assured or Guaranteed Returns

  • Clients must not be promised assured, guaranteed, minimum, or percentage-based returns.
  • Statements like "service will continue till target returns are achieved" are strictly prohibited.

2. No Loss Recovery Assurances

  • Clients must not be told that losses will be recovered in the future if they continue or engage with our services.

3. Limited Communication Channels

  • Investment calls, tips, or advice must not be offered through WhatsApp or other social media platforms.
  • **Recommendations must only be sent via SMS**, as per policy.

4. Voluntary Payments Only

  • Clients must not be coerced, misled, or emotionally influenced to make payments.
  • Payments should only be requested when the client is genuinely interested in the service.

5. No Account or Portfolio Management

  • We are not permitted to directly manage client accounts or portfolios under any circumstance.

6. No Misleading Performance Claims

  • Past performance must not be used to imply future accuracy or returns.
  • Claims such as "80-90% of our recommendations are profitable" cannot be made without documented, verifiable proof.

7. Avoid Misleading or Risk-Masking Statements

  • No recommendation should be labeled as "**safe**," "**risk-free**," or similar.
  • Comments about the nature or quality of any trade must be avoided.

8. No Misrepresentation or Exaggeration

  • Statements that could mislead or exploit an investor's lack of knowledge must not be made.
  • Exaggerated claims or overstatements of our capabilities, achievements, or qualifications are not allowed.

9. No Complex or Confusing Language

  • Communication must be in simple, easy-to-understand language. Avoid technical jargon or complex phrasing that may confuse or mislead investors.

10. No False "Free Service" Claims

  • A service should only be called free if it is genuinely and fully free.

11. No Competitor Disparagement

  • Comparisons with competitors, especially those that discredit or unfairly criticize their performance, are strictly prohibited.

12. No personalised Recommendations

  • Recommendations sent to customer cannot be represented as personalized or tailored.
